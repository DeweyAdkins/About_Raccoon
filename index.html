About Raccoons

Raccoons are medium-sized mammals native to North America. They are known for their dexterous front paws, masked faces, and ringed tails.

Habitat

Raccoons are highly adaptable and can be found in a variety of habitats, including forests, marshes, and urban areas.

Behavior

Raccoons are nocturnal and omnivorous, known for their intelligence and ability to open containers and doors to find food.
